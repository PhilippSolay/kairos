Importance — cost of never doing it

  • 5 revenue / live commitment — rare
  • 4 moves a real company goal
  • 3 matters, survives a month — your default
  • 2 marginal
  • 1 trivial

Leave blank → inherits the Category default. Set only to override.

Urgency — cost of doing it later

  • 5 overdue / blocking someone / window closing
  • 4 due in days
  • 3 this week
  • 2 can wait
  • 1 whenever

The clock & other people — not your stress. Hard date? Put it in Deadline.

Effort — size of the next action

  • 30m–1h a real task
  • 2h borderline
  • 4h+ that's a project → split, board only the first step

It's the divisor: small + important floats to the top. Don't pad everything to one size.

Score = (Imp×2 + Urg×1.5 + deadline) ÷ Effort. Spread the numbers, or nothing ranks.
